SEO Myths & Truths

Myth: SEO is just about keywords

Truth: Modern SEO is a technical and strategic process. Keywords matter, but structure, speed, user experience, and authority are equally critical.

Myth: Backlinks alone can rank your site

Truth: Backlinks help, but Google prioritizes sites with clean architecture, optimized content, and credible references.

Myth: SEO is instant

Truth: SEO builds a digital asset over time. Technical optimizations and quality content compound results over months.

Myth: Duplicate content doesn't matter

Truth: Duplicate content confuses search engines and can lower your ranking. Original, structured content is critical.

Myth: SEO is only about Google

Truth: While Google dominates, modern SEO considers user intent, mobile experience, and multiple search platforms.

Myth: SEO is cheating the algorithm

Truth: True SEO is engineering your website for humans and search engines simultaneously. It's about trust and relevance, not tricks.
